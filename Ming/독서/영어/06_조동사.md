## Point
#### can => 능력, 가능성, 허락
```
1) She can speak English.
2) It can be good to meet him.
3) You can smoke here.
```
#### could
- can의 과거
```
She said (that) it could be good to meet him.
```
- can보다 가능성이 적을 떄
```
It could be good to meet him.
```
#### should => 충고, 권유, 도덕성
- 나의 의지 개입! (하면 좋은데 안 해도 됨)
#### must => 의무
- 나의 의지 개입 불가능! (반드시 해야되는 것)
```
You [should / must] breathe to survive.
You [should / must] pay the property tax bill.
You [should / must] consume plenty of water in the summer.
```
#### will => 미래에 있을 확실한 일, 의지
```
He will make a public statement tomorrow.
She will pass the test.
```
#### would
- will의 과거
```
I thought (that) she would pass the test
```
- will보다 확실성이 떨어질 때
```
It would be bery expensive to stay there.
```
#### may => 불확실한 가능서으 약한 추측
```
I'm not sure, but he may be a student.
```
#### might
- may의 과거
```
I thought (that) she might be a student.
```
- may에 비해 확실성 떨어짐(주로 가정의 상황)
```
I might clean the room.
```
## 팁!!
#### Shall 답이 아닐 가능성이 높다!
#### 동물 주어인 경우 can일 가능성이 높다!
#### (in order) to 부정사가 나오는 경우 should 또는 must일 가능성이 높다!
